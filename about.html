About Me

Greetings everyone!! My name is Jamie and I’ve been into music since I was a kid. It’s always been a dream of mine to be a famous rapper, in fact, I have many songs I’ve wrote and recorded, also have done music videos and put them all over social media under the name “J Breaze Pittsburgh rapper” but another dream of mine was to be a DJ!

We’ve all heard “things happen for a reason, right!

Well, while doing my music videos, I was introduced to the right people who led me into what I need to do and what I need to get to be a professional DJ because I already had the personality and professionalism! I also feel that’s why you’re here now, reading this, you’ve somehow been led my way and now “we” are in connection with the music! It’s great how things work out! So let’s set up a 15-30 minute phone call to discuss your special day I’d be delighted being a part of!
